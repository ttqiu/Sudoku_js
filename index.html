  • Input numbers 1-9 and press Enter. If the number is repeating, the number will turn red when inputting.
  • Numbers 1-9 must be filled out in each row, column, and 3x3 square without repeating any numbers.
  • If you're unsure of what number to put in a particular space, move on, continue scanning the other grid areas and come back later.
  • Try eliminating invalid numbers by checking which other numbers are already included within each square, row, or column.
  • To confirm if the puzzle has been solved, click the Check button.
  • Having trouble figuring out the Sudoku puzzle? To view the solution, click the Solve button.
